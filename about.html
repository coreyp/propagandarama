What is this?

Propagandarama is a free online translation service, similar to Google Translate or BabelFish. However, instead of translating, say, English to Spanish or Greek to Portugese, Propagandarama takes misleading, decontextualized, TK and otherwise propagandistic material from various media sources and translates it into the simplest possible English terminology.

For instance.

Peruse the home page for the latest translation, and the archive page for a selection of past material. Wave your cursor over the original propaganda to reveal the translation, a list of relevant terms and a link to the original source.

(Propagandarama works best on a computer equipped with a mouse or trackpad. Results may vary on a touch-screen device.)
