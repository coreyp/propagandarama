What is this?

Propagandarama is like Google Translate or BabelFish—with a difference.

Propagandarama doesn't merely render English into Spanish or Greek into Portugese or Mandarin into Persian. Our bespoke algorithm takes misleading, decontextualized, euphemistic, emotion-driven, assumption-laden and otherwise propagandistic material from various media sources and translates it into the simplest possible English.
