What is this?

Propagandarama is a free service that translates propaganda from various sources into the simplest possible English terms.For instance.

Find the latest selection of propaganda on the home page. Simply wave your cursor over the words on the page to reveal the translation, a list of relevant terms and a link to the source. (To use Propagandarama on a touch-screen mobile device, tap the desired text; however, the site works best on a computer equiped with a mouse or trackpad.)
